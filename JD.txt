**Job Description: Data Scientist**  

**Location:** Remote / Hybrid / Onsite  
**Employment Type:** Full-Time  

**About the Role:**  
We are seeking a Data Scientist with expertise in machine learning, data analytics, and AI-driven decision-making. The ideal candidate will develop predictive models, build data pipelines, and apply NLP techniques to extract insights and optimize business outcomes.  

**Key Responsibilities:**  
- Develop predictive models to enhance business decision-making.  
- Apply NLP techniques for text analysis and insights.  
- Build and maintain data pipelines for efficient data processing.  
- Utilize Python, SQL, R, TensorFlow, and Scikit-Learn for model development.  
- Create reports and visualizations using Tableau, Pandas, and NumPy.  

**Qualifications:**  
- 4+ years of experience in machine learning and analytics.  
- M.Sc. in Data Science, Computer Science, or a related field.  
- Strong programming skills in Python, R, and SQL.  
- Experience with recommendation systems and fraud detection models.
